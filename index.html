LEADERSHIP

PASTOR EDWARD A LANHAM

“Behold my servant, whom I have chosen; my beloved, in whom my soul is well pleased: I will poor my Spirit upon him, Matt. 12:18.” Serving God as he serves the church, Pastor Edward Lanham is anointed to serve. With a unique love for and dedication to the church, Pastor Lanham serves as the Senior Pastor of the Fishers of Men Church Of God In Christ Family Worship Center located in Mastic Beach, New York.

This visionary leader is an anointed teacher, motivator, and a powerful preacher whose thrust for ministry is focused on strengthening the family with prayer as the foundation built upon the Word. Through messages of hope, deliverance and victory, Pastor Lanham promotes the Gospel as the standard for practical living.

Early in his ministry the Lord spoke to Pastor Lanham directing him that God would use him to encourage souls to build their lives on Christ the Solid Rock. Pastor Lanham has served the Church of God in Christ on the local, district, jurisdictional and national levels from usher board member to Sunday School Superintendent, Evangelist, District Executive Secretary, and National Adjutant Overseer to name a few.

Compassionate for the success of men, Brother Encourage Thyself, was birthed to spiritually empower men as iron sharpeneth iron. As the CEO of Eastern Long Island St Paul Community Development Inc, a 501-C3 Not for Profit, Pastor Lanham engages in social ministries to build our community with economic development including housing development, crime reduction, and financial literacy.

ELECT LADY JEAN LANHAM

Elect Lady Jean Michele Lanham has walked faithfully beside her one and only husband of 32 years, Supt. Edward Lanham, where they have served in their local ministry at Fishers Of Men since 1995 as well as within the O. M. Kelly Memorial District, Eastern New York Third Jurisdiction and the National Church of God in Christ. She and her husband work together in their local ministry to serve our families, and communities as we serve God as an effective change in lives to empower believers as they pursue their purpose, fulfill their destiny and have dominion as is spoken in the Bible.

Elect Lady Jean Michele Lanham holds an Associates Degree in Business Administration and Early Childhood Development. Lady Lanham is a Notary Public and works as the Office Assistant of Athletics for Center Moriches School District. Lady Lanham oversees the Women Dept at Fishers of Men COGIC and established the Woman of Judah ministries to encourage women of all stages of life in their walk with God, with a mission to help strengthen women in their relationship with God through Prayer, the Word and fellowship with one another.

Elect Lady Jean Michele Lanham is a third generation COGIC member (COGIC women rock). Lady Lanham was licensed as a Missionary Evangelist in the Church of God in Christ in 19___ under the leadership of the late Bishop Frank Otha White, General Board Member and Eastern New York 3rd Jurisdictional Prelate and the late Mother Evelyn Green, ENY3 Supervisor of Women. Lady Lanham currently serves on the Executive Women’s Committee within the Women’s Department of Eastern New York 3 Jurisdiction where Bishop Frank A White is the Jurisdictional Prelate and Mother Jannie McGhee, Supervisor of Women Designee and Dr. Yashima White Azilove, Asst. Supervisor of Women Designee. Lady Lanham is also a member of the National Adjutancy of the Church Of God In Christ.

Elect Lady Jean Michele Lanham is a member of the Brookhaven NAACP where she serves as the Committee Chairperson for Finance. Lady Lanham is founder of the 501c3 corporation Helping Hands Healing Hearts Outreach Ministry which was to go out into the highway and hedges with Helping Hands to Heal Hurting Hearts with a purpose to be a vessel for the building of the Kingdom according to 1 John 3:17 in the community where Fishers Of Men COGIC is located. Lady Lanham has aligned the Helping Hands Healing Hearts Outreach ministry with Long Island Cares and has run an active food pantry for five years.

Elect Lady Jean Michele Lanham is a true servant-leader and has touched the lives of her four children: Erik (Johnnel), Edward Donald (Betsy), Emmanuel and Elisabeth, as well as her seven grandchildren: Cayenne, Zion, Alexis, Zara, Tehya, Aiyana and Zaihre. All of their children have served in the US Military of which she is extremely proud and have begun their career paths.
